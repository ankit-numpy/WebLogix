Our Journey

Founded in 2020, Mantra WebLogix started with a simple goal: to make web development more accessible and efficient. Over the years, we've grown into a team of passionate developers and designers, helping businesses of all sizes achieve their digital goals.

Our flagship product, TripContriSplitter (TCS), was born out of a real need during our team's travel experiences. We realized how cumbersome it was to track and settle group expenses, so we built a solution that makes it simple and transparent.
